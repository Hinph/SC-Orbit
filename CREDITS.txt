SC-ORBIT CREDITS
================

SC-Orbit grew from the Steam Controller support pioneered by SC-Wrapper and its
contributors. Their original implementation made this project possible.

SC-Orbit substantially expands and rewrites the installer, targeted physical
device takeover, virtual-device architecture, controller-first hotkeys, fluid
trackball and spinner processing, gyro systems, number pad, profiles, and haptic
feedback.

Please preserve credit to SC-Wrapper and its contributors in redistributions or
derivative work.
